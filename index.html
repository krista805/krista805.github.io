About me

My name is Krista Simmons I’m a freelance web developer based in San Diego. My passions lie within music, snowboarding, technology and basically everything involving the internet. My technical skills include (but not limited to) building custom Wordpress & Shopify websites for small to medium sized businesses. Currently my toolbelt consists of Javascript, Vue.js/Nuxt, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Tailwind, PHP. From my time working at digital agencies, I have gained knowledge in User Experience (UX) design, ADA/Web Accessibility best practices and search engine optimization (SEO). My work as a freelance web developer stands apart because of my ability to explain and execute technical concepts simply, while maintaining a friendly & productive relationship with clients.
